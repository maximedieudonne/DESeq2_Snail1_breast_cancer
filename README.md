# DESeq2_Snail1_breast_cancer
Demo study : transcription factor snail1 in triple negative breast cancer cells.
This study is based on the article : Maturi, V., Morén, A., Enroth, S., Heldin, C., & Moustakas, A. (2018). Genomewide binding of transcription factor Snail1 in triple‐negative breast cancer cells. Molecular Oncology, 12(7), 1153‑1174. https://doi.org/10.1002/1878-0261.12317.
These demo study is a training from the video of the channel OMGenomics : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NGbZmlGLG5w&list=PLevQaddMWNQzkuh1-908nfoTlUYesTfFk&index=8
